# Privacy Policy for Sudoku Saga

**Last Updated: February 3, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to Sudoku Saga ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Sudoku Saga mobile application (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the App.

## Information We Collect

### Information We Do NOT Collect

Sudoku Saga is designed with privacy in mind. We do **NOT** collect:
- Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number)
- Location data
- Contact lists
- Photos or camera access
- Social media profile information
- Any personally identifiable information

### Information Automatically Collected

When you use the App, certain information may be automatically collected:

#### Device Information
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- Mobile network information

#### Usage Data
- Game statistics (levels completed, time spent, scores)
- In-app purchase history
- App performance data
- Crash reports and diagnostics

#### Local Storage
All game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using:
- SQLite database for game statistics and level progress
- MMKV storage for app settings and user preferences
- This data never leaves your device unless you uninstall the app

We use the automatically collected information for the following purposes:

### Game Functionality
- Save your game progress and statistics
- Track daily challenges and streaks
- Manage hint inventory and in-app purchases
- Provide personalized game experience

### Analytics and Improvement
- Understand how users interact with the App
- Identify and fix bugs and crashes
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Analyze usage patterns to enhance features

### In-App Purchases

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All game data is stored locally on your device
- Data is not transmitted to external servers
- Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all local data

### Security Measures
We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:
- Secure communication with third-party services (HTTPS/TLS)
- No storage of sensitive personal information
- Regular security updates and patches

However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Your Rights and Choices

### Data Access and Deletion
- **Access**: All your game data is stored locally and accessible within the App
- **Deletion**: Uninstall the App to permanently delete all local data
- **Reset**: Use the in-app settings to reset game progress

### Opt-Out Options
- **Analytics**: You cannot opt-out of basic analytics required for app functionality
- **Ads**: You can choose not to watch rewarded ads (if enabled)
- **Purchases**: You can choose not to make in-app purchases

### Advertising Choices
If ads are enabled, you can:
- Limit ad tracking in your device settings (iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising; Android: Settings > Google > Ads)

- Opt-out of personalized ads
